Vinayaka" is another name for the Hindu deity Ganesha, also known as Ganapati or Vighneshwara. He is widely revered as the god of beginnings, wisdom, and success, and is often worshipped before commencing any new venture or undertaking. The name "Vinayaka" is used in various Hindu scriptures like the Puranas and in Buddhist Tantras.
